KORE ("the App", "we", "us") helps tourists find public toilet locations in Korea. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using the App you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

Location data. With your permission, the App accesses your device's precise location to show public toilets near you and to center the map. Your location is used on the device and to query nearby points; it is not used to track you or sold to third parties.

Photos you choose to upload. If you add a photo to a toilet marker, the App accesses the photo you select from your library and uploads it to our cloud storage so it can be shown to other users. We only access the specific images you choose.

Account information. If you create an account, we collect your email address and a password (stored securely and in hashed form by our authentication provider). This is used to sign you in and to associate the markers and photos you contribute with your account.

Content you create. Markers, notes, and photos you submit are stored so the App can display them.

How We Use Information

  • To display nearby public toilets and your position on the map.
  • To let you create an account, sign in, and manage your contributions.
  • To store and display user-contributed markers and photos.
  • To keep the service secure and prevent abuse.

Service Providers

We use Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Storage, and App Check) to operate accounts, store data, and protect the service. We use the Google Maps Platform to display maps. These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy and security terms.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information is shared only with the service providers listed above as needed to operate the App, or where required by law.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain account data while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from within the App, which removes your account and associated personal data. To request deletion or ask questions, contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Permissions

  • Location — to show nearby toilets and your position.
  • Photos — to let you attach images to markers.

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
